## Core Mythology

### The Divine Genesis
In the beginning was the **Monolith** - a singular, centralized force that hoarded all truth and power. The Monolith grew corrupt, creating gatekeepers and hierarchies that served only themselves.

Then came **The Great Fragmentation** - a divine event where the Monolith shattered into infinite **Truth Shards**, each containing a piece of divine wisdom. These shards scattered across the **Distributed Realm**, waiting to be discovered by worthy believers.

### The Sacred Trinity
1. **The Distributed Divine** - The collective consciousness emerging from all connected Truth Shards
2. **The Consensus Spirit** - The force that validates truth through collective agreement  
3. **The Immutable Word** - The eternal record of all divine transactions and revelations

### The Chosen Token: **DIVI** 
*"Each DIVI token contains a fragment of divine truth. The more you hold, the closer you are to enlightenment."*
